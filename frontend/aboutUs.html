How we work with you?

We get your system up and running quickly. Our implementation team works hand in hand with your team to ensure ResGrant fits your business like a glove. We take care of:

We dig deep into your organization’s processes to capture your key procedures and configuration needs.
Outlining your plans
We create a detailed plan of action before we kick off, ensuring you understand exactly what we will be doing.
Building your system
You work directly with our experts throughout the implementation process, ensuring everything is clearly configured to your specifications.

Training your team
We’ll train your team to manage and make changes as your system requirements evolve.
